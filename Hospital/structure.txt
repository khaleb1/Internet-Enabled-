### 1. **Appointment Booking System:**
   - **Patient Interface:** Allow patients to book appointments with doctors.
   - **Doctor Availability:** Ensure doctors can set their availability.
   - **Conflict Checking:** Implement logic to prevent double-booking.

### 2. **User Management:**
   - **Registration:** Ensure patients and doctors can register.
   - **Profile Management:** Allow users to update their profiles.

### 3. **Notification System:**
   - **Email/SMS Alerts:** Notify patients and doctors about upcoming appointments.
   - **Reminders:** Send reminders for appointments.

### 4. **Reporting and Analytics:**
   - **Admin Dashboard:** Provide insights into system usage, such as the number of appointments, active users, etc.
   - **Export Data:** Allow exporting of reports in CSV or PDF format.

### 5. **Security Enhancements:**
   - **Password Reset:** Implement a password reset feature.
   - **Role-Based Access Control:** Ensure users have access only to their respective areas.

### 6. **Feedback and Support:**
   - **Feedback Form:** Allow users to provide feedback on their experience.
   - **Support System:** Implement a basic support ticket system.

### 7. **Testing and Optimization:**
   - **Unit Tests:** Write tests for critical parts of your application.
   - **Performance Optimization:** Optimize database queries and page load times.
